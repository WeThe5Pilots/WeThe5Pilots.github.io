Overview

"FLYERS" is a paper plane building program hosted to foster the art of plane building and explain the basic concept of aeronautical science behind airplanes.

The paper plane workshop and contest is a fun way of learning the art of plane design. Build and Be the Pilot of your paper plane and win exciting prizes too.
